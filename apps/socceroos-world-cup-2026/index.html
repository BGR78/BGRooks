Model notes

Forecasts use only scores entered as completed FIFA World Cup 2026 matches. They do not use betting odds, squad strength, Elo, historical results or FIFA rankings to predict match scores.

The displayed forecast is a mean/median blend of Ben's four score components. The app also keeps the pure mean and pure median to show a rough range.

FIFA rankings are not used for prediction. They can be used later only if needed to reproduce an official FIFA tiebreak or allocation rule.
