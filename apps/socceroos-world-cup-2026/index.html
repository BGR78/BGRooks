Model notes

Forecasts use only scores entered as completed FIFA World Cup 2026 matches. They do not use betting odds, squad strength, Elo, historical results or FIFA rankings to predict match scores.

The displayed forecast is the median of Ben's eight spreadsheet components: each team's scoring/conceding averages, opponent averages, additive performance modifiers, and percentage performance modifiers. The displayed range uses the first and third quartiles of those same components.

FIFA rankings are not used for prediction. The confirmed Round of 32 bracket is fixed in this version, so the app no longer tries to infer third-place allocations itself. In knockout draws, penalty winners affect advancement only; the tied score remains the evidence used by the forecast model.
